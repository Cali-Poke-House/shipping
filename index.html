How this works 👋

Quick Pack — your main page. Pack a box, then tap +1 on its stream, or hit 📷 Scan a Label to Start and scan each label. The board keeps your count for you.

Team Count — for busy, all-hands days. Tap your name first, then tap +1 or scan — everyone adds into one shared total. Someone from another team jumps in? Hit + Add helper so they show up too.

Team — a big-screen view of the whole crew: total shipped, the goal, and which platforms still have work left.

The number under your name is just yours — only you see your own card.
